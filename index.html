About Flyxa

A better trip starts before you click book.

Good travel tools do not ask you to juggle tabs, prices, and email chains. Flyxa keeps the moving parts calm, even when the trip is not.

AI itineraries that adjust when the trip does

Change the city, shift the layover, miss a train. Flyxa rewrites the plan around what is still possible instead of forcing you to start over.

Price prediction that tells you when to wait

Flyxa watches fare movement and gives you a straight read. Book now, hold off, or move fast before the number climbs.

One dashboard for every booking and every change

Flights, stays, timing updates, and check-in details live in one clean view. You know what changed and what still needs a decision.
